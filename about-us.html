Our Story

Freshly squeezed since 2025.
Georgetown Juicery was founded in 2025 by Lisa Foreman and Merlin Sebastian in Georgetown, DC. They brought their vision to life of creating smoothies, juices, and more from wholesome ingredients. Ingredients are sourced from local farmers, making Georgetown Juicery the place for feel-good food and beverages.
